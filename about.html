Ankuram Foundation is a registered non-profit organization committed to advancing the welfare and empowerment of children and girls. Guided by the vision and leadership of our President, Ms. Anita Singh, the Foundation works tirelessly to safeguard the rights and well-being of some of the most vulnerable sections of society.

Our core focus areas include child protection, education, healthcare, gender equality, and the prevention of child labour and child marriage. Through a range of sustainable programs and community-based interventions, we strive to create safer, healthier, and more equitable opportunities for children and young girls to grow and flourish.

At Ankuram Foundation, we believe that every child deserves a secure childhood, quality education, and equal access to opportunities that enable them to realize their fullest potential. Our team works in close collaboration with communities, local authorities, and partner organizations to bring about meaningful and lasting change.
